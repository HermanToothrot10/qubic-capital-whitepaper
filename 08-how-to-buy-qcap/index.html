    How to Buy $QCAP

    $QCAP is acquired through two complementary markets.

    Primary Market

    In the Primary Market, new $QCAP tokens are issued directly by Qubic Capital through governance-approved fundraising rounds executed in QVAULT.

    Each round specifies:

    • The amount of $QCAP to be issued
    • The price per token

    These parameters are decided collectively by eligible voters.

    Participation in the Primary Market directly strengthens Qubic Capital, as the capital raised is allocated to Smart Contract acquisitions, ecosystem initiatives, and revenue-generating opportunities. This expands the portfolio and reinforces long-term ecosystem growth.

    Primary rounds are conducted at a fixed price defined in the proposal, ensuring equal conditions for all participants.

    Secondary Market

    $QCAP is also acquired through peer-to-peer trading or QX (Qubic Exchange).

    The Secondary Market provides liquidity and flexibility, allowing participants to adjust their positions at any time. Prices are determined by supply and demand.

    While the Secondary Market enhances tradability and visibility, it does not directly fund Qubic Capital. In contrast, the Primary Market injects new capital into the ecosystem and expands the underlying portfolio base.

    Both markets complement each other:

    • Primary Market drives capital formation and portfolio expansion
    • Secondary Market provides liquidity and price discovery
