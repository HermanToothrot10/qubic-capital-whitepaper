# The Qubic Capital Solution

**Smaller investors pool their resources to invest collectively**, creating an investment structure that enables access to opportunities that might be out of reach individually. By joining forces, these investors diversify their investments and share potential returns.

Qubic Capital enables participants to take part in Initial Public Offerings, providing access to early-stage Smart Contract shares. It also enables participation in the acquisition of high-priced Smart Contract shares in the secondary market, offering broader exposure beyond primary issuances. This structure allows individual investors to **benefit from both early-stage and secondary market opportunities** that might otherwise be reserved for larger players.

In addition, Qubic Capital contributes to the **development of DeFi (decentralized finance) within the Qubic ecosystem**. Qbond is the first solution powered by Qubic Capital, designed to support the broader ecosystem while generating additional revenue streams. These revenues contribute to the overall performance of Qubic Capital and provide extra value to $QCAP holders.
