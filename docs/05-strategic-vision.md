# Strategic Vision

Qubic Capital positions itself as the **benchmark investment vehicle** within the Qubic ecosystem, serving as the primary gateway to diversified, ecosystem-wide exposure. By allocating capital across multiple revenue-generating Smart Contracts and strategic initiatives, it operates as an ETF-like structure that provides systemic participation in the growth of the ecosystem.

Unlike single-asset exposure strategies, Qubic Capital offers **diversified systemic exposure through a single token**.

Qubic Capital already represents a significant share of dividend-generating Smart Contract ownership within the ecosystem, reinforcing its benchmark status. Its ambition is to continue expanding this participation over time, strengthening portfolio depth and broadening exposure across the most relevant initiatives within Qubic.

By holding $QCAP, participants gain access to revenue generated by the underlying portfolio, including Smart Contract dividends and DeFi products powered by Qubic Capital. In addition to these recurring revenues, holders may also benefit from the appreciation of the token price as the portfolio expands and ecosystem adoption grows. The token reflects the performance of the portfolio, aligning holders with long-term ecosystem growth.
