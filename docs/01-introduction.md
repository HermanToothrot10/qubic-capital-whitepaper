# Introduction

Qubic Capital enables everyone, regardless of the size of their portfolio, to **own shares in smart contracts while maintaining diversification**. Through this project, members have the opportunity to participate in Qubic’s IPO, an event typically reserved for the wealthiest investors, and to become co-owners of an extensive portfolio, the largest available, of shares representing the entire Qubic ecosystem.

Investment decisions are subject to a majority vote by eligible members before implementation. All participants can become eligible members with voting power, with voting weight determined by their holdings and participation in governance.

Governance, capital allocation, fundraising, and revenue distribution are managed automatically and in a decentralized manner through **QVAULT**, the smart contract that administers all operational aspects of Qubic Capital. This approach ensures that all participants who engage in governance have a say in the direction of the project.
