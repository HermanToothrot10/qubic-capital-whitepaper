# Tokenomics

## Supply

The total maximum supply of $QCAP equals **21,000,000 tokens**, of which **3,000,000 tokens** form part of the reserve.

The emission schedule adjusts according to market conditions, the broader cryptocurrency cycle, and the development of Qubic. Decisions on issuance are determined by holders through governance proposals and votes executed in QVAULT.

New $QCAP tokens are released exclusively through governance-approved fundraising rounds. There are no automatic emissions or uncontrolled supply increases.

Each new issuance is tied to a defined capital allocation plan, typically participation in upcoming IPOs or the acquisition of additional revenue-generating assets. The capital raised is deployed directly into portfolio expansion.

This mechanism prevents economic dilution. **When new tokens are issued, the increase in supply is matched by a corresponding increase in underlying portfolio assets.** Token issuance therefore represents capitalization of new assets rather than redistribution of existing value, maintaining alignment between circulating supply and intrinsic portfolio value per token.

## Reserve

The reserved allocation serves strategic purposes such as ecosystem alignment, incentives, partnerships, or long-term development. The specific use of the reserve is determined by holders through proposals and votes in QVAULT.

## Revenue Distribution

Revenue distribution is structured as follows:

- **3% goes to QVAULT smart contract shareholders, immutable**
- The remaining 97% is allocated among distribution to QCAP holders, the reinvestment fund, and QCAP burn, not available before 1 January 2029

Revenue generated from the assets held by Qubic Capital is redistributed directly in $QUBIC. Redistribution occurs at the end of each epoch, on Wednesday at 12:00 UTC, and is made to holders existing at the end of the epoch in proportion to their eligible balance.

## Portfolio Valuation Framework

Qubic Capital maintains a structured and transparent valuation framework for its underlying portfolio.

Portfolio valuation follows these principles:

- Asset-level valuation based on observable market prices on QX
- Use of averaged recent trades to reduce short-term volatility
- Inclusion of all held Smart Contract shares, DeFi positions, and cash balances
- Periodic disclosure of total portfolio value and circulating supply

The intrinsic value per token is derived by dividing the net portfolio value attributable to $QCAP holders by the circulating supply.

This framework ensures transparency, consistency, and traceability in how the value of $QCAP is assessed over time.
