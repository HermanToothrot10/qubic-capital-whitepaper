# Long-Term Alignment

Qubic Capital is structured to align governance, capital formation, and revenue generation under a single coordinated framework.

Holders participate in decision-making through QVAULT, capital raised through approved fundraising expands the portfolio, and revenues generated by underlying assets flow back to holders according to predefined distribution rules. This creates structural alignment between supply growth and portfolio expansion.

As the Qubic ecosystem evolves, Qubic Capital aims to remain a benchmark vehicle for diversified exposure, capital coordination, and long-term participation in ecosystem growth.

The model is designed for **sustainability rather than short-term speculation**, reinforcing alignment between holders and portfolio development over time.
